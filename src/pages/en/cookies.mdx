Information about the use of cookies and trackers on the Tatikati app and our website.

## 1. What is a cookie?

<HighlightBox title="Technical definition">
A cookie is a small text file placed on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when visiting a website or using an application. It allows your device to be recognized during subsequent visits.
</HighlightBox>

### Types of cookies used

- **🍪 Session cookies** - Temporary, deleted when the application is closed
- **📅 Persistent cookies** - Kept until expiration or manual deletion
- **🥇 First-party cookies** - Placed directly by Tatikati
- **🤝 Third-party cookies** - Placed by our partners (with your consent)

- **🔵 Google Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies

- **🧭 Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect the application's functionality. Essential cookies cannot be disabled.

## 7. Your rights

Regarding cookies and the data they collect, you have the following rights:

- **🔍 Right to information** - Be informed about cookie use before placement
- **✋ Right to object** - Refuse non-essential cookies at any time
- **🗑️ Right to erasure** - Request deletion of data collected via cookies
- **⚙️ Right to configuration** - Precisely configure your cookie preferences
- **📋 Right of access** - Know which cookies are placed and their purposes
- **🔄 Right to modification** - Change your preferences at any time

### How to exercise your rights?

- **In the application:** Settings → Privacy → Cookies
- **By email:** [contact@helby.studio](mailto:contact@helby.studio)
- **Via our form:** Contact section of our website
